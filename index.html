About Me

I am a researcher at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, working on 6D single-molecule orientation and localization microscopy (6D-SMOLM). My research focuses on developing deep learning frameworks that combine multiple neural networks to simultaneously determine both 3D spatial positions and 3D orientations of fluorescent molecules.

I develop custom microscopy systems using double-helix point spread functions and polarization-resolved detection, coupled with advanced computational methods for super-resolution imaging and molecular orientation analysis.
